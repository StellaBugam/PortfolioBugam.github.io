My Expertise

Data collection

As the lead data analyst, the first step involves gathering and connecting to diverse data sources. I primarily use Power Query in Power BI to extract, transform, and load (ETL) data from tools like Excel (as a database) and MongoDB. Power Query enables me to clean and shape raw data by merging tables, filtering unnecessary rows, and creating calculated columns directly within the Power BI environment. This process ensures that the data is well-structured, accurate, and ready for analysis. My ability to seamlessly integrate multiple data sources allows me to build a solid foundation for deeper insights.

Data Processing

Once the data is collected, I focus on transforming it into an analysis-ready format. Using DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) in Power BI, I create measures and calculated columns that enable advanced calculations such as aggregations, trends, and comparisons. DAX allows me to derive meaningful insights by defining custom formulas that go beyond basic analysis. This stage ensures that the data model is robust, relationships between tables are properly defined, and all necessary transformations are complete for effective visualization.

Data Visualization

The final step of my workflow is crafting interactive dashboards and reports in Power BI to communicate insights effectively. By leveraging Power BI's extensive visualization capabilities such as bar charts, line graphs, slicers, and drill-through features I create intuitive reports tailored to the audience's needs. These dashboards allow stakeholders to explore key metrics dynamically while maintaining clarity through consistent formatting and storytelling techniques. My visualizations are designed not only to present data but also to guide decision-makers toward actionable insights by highlighting trends, patterns, and anomalies in a visually engaging way.

Testing und validation

I have a diversified expertise in the planning and implementation of testing and validation processes aimed at ensuring that products or systems fully meet the needs and requirements of patients or clients. This approach involves conducting rigorous functional tests, meticulous evaluations, and thorough verifications to ensure compliance with established standards of performance, safety, and quality, thereby ensuring a high level of stakeholder satisfaction.

Latest Project

ADAS Perfomance Dashboard

The ADAS Performance Dashboard has played a key role in providing actionable insights into the effectiveness of advanced driver assistance systems across multiple vehicle models and test campaigns. Widely used by engineering teams and project managers, the dashboard enables rapid identification of trends in emergency braking, lane keeping, and adaptive cruise control performance. Notable outcomes include:

  • Improved detection and resolution of system anomalies during testing phases
  • Enhanced collaboration between test engineers and software developers
  • Accelerated validation cycles for new ADAS features

Through this project, I learned the value of clear data visualization for communicating technical results to diverse stakeholders.

Test Coverage Dashboard

Personal Projet

This project allowed me to apply my skills in business intelligence, data analysis, and analytical design.

This project strengthened my skills in test automation, data validation, and quality assurance for data-centric applications.
